Complimentary

Color scheme and typography complimentary toward your existing materials and the Lake Forest site. Grays and red come from existing DC logos. Gold comes from the acorns in the Lake Forest logo. The font used is the Google font Muli, which is a close match for Avenir, the font used on the Lake Forest site.

Quiet

Not a significant departure from the Complimentary scheme. We introduce a second Google font, GFS Didot. It adds some contrast as a serifed font, and a little more of a historic feel. Colors are the same (excepting the gold), but used in a more muted approach.
